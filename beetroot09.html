China - a vast and diverse country - exhibits remarkable differences in its economic landscape.
As we learn more about China, we find that different regions contribute to the country's economy in unique ways.
Contrasting between these regions, from the bustling metropolises that drive high GDP growth to the regions with fast-growing incomes, paints a complete picture of China's economy.
Understanding these regional differences not only provides insight into China's current economic health, but also identifies potential areas of growth and opportunity.
